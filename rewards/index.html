FAQ

What do my friends (or anyone you referred to us) receive?

With your code they pick a complimentary EyeSTAR Suite stay or €50 off their surgery.

Do ineligible candidates count?

Yes. If someone is medically ineligible after exam, they count as 0.5 towards your total. Maximum of 2 medically ineligible referral can be used towards your goal.  

How is progress tracked?

Your referrals are recorded in our clinic's cloud system  . You can check your progress on the progress page using your code.

What if I’m not eligible for LASIK?

If your exam shows you aren’t a candidate, your reward can be transferred to someone you choose for up to 2 years.

How long does it take?

Most ambassadors get their 5 referrals within months. You have up to 2 years total to reach your goal. If you reach less than 5, you may use your referrals for discount towards your lasik treatment as well.

Can I transfer my reward?

Yes. You can transfer your reward to anyone you choose. You can gift it, sell it, it is up to you. But you need to inform us before hand.

Program Terms

  • Referral means any person (friend, family member, colleague, or even someone you do not personally know) whom you help to have a LASIK treatment at EyeSTAR using your unique ID code provided here.
  • Please obtain only one ID code and use the same ID code for all of your referrals.
  • You win free LASIK + an EyeSTAR Suite stay after 5 completed LASIK referrals recorded with your code.
  • Referrals must be new patients with new appointments in order to be counted toward your referral goal (not someone who has already booked an appointment with us).
  • All standard laser vision correction treatments such as LASIK, LASEK, PRK and No-Touch will be free with 5 referrals.
  • Special laser operations such as Z-LASIK require an extra €499, or are free with a total of 8 referrals. Contoura is an additional €999, or free with a total of 10 referrals. Z-Contoura is an additional €1,299, or free with a total of 12 referrals.
  • You have a total of 2 years from your signup date to reach 5 referrals. If the program continues, you may ask to have your existing referrals carried over into the new term.
  • Up to two medically ineligible candidates (after our examination) count as 0.5 referral each.
  • If you can’t wait to reach 5 referrals, you may use referrals as discounts: €150 per qualifying referral.
  • Reverse option: you may have surgery now at full price and send referrals later. The calculation and process for this option are different; please contact us if you would like to choose this path.
  • LASIK, PRK, LASEK, Z-LASIK, No-Touch, SMILE PRO, Contoura and Z-Contoura each count as 1 referral.
  • Clinical eligibility and standard medical criteria apply to all surgeries. Final approval is always based on our medical examination and safety guidelines.
